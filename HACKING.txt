HACKING on TennisFightingGame
=============================


Style
-----

* Try and follow standard C# conventions (the Microsoft ones)

* Don't use post 3.0-ish syntactic sugar, it's literraly unreadable

* Stick to 100 characters per line if possible. Don't go over 120.

* Stupid OOP is not allowed
